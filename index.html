FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, treatments, and clinic policies.

View All FAQs

What conditions does Tadawi Clinic treat?

We treat a wide range of musculoskeletal and neurological conditions including back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, ACL rehabilitation, post-surgery recovery, stroke rehabilitation, shoulder pain, sciatica, disc problems, knee pain, and more.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book through our website, WhatsApp, phone call, or Facebook page. We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you do not need a referral. You can book directly. However, if you have one, please bring it along with any relevant medical records.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring any medical reports, imaging results (X-rays, MRI), a list of current medications, and wear comfortable clothing suitable for physical examination and exercises.
