Our Story

A Journey of Healing & Innovation

Tadawi Clinic was founded with a singular vision: to redefine the standard of physical therapy and rehabilitation in Egypt. What began as a small practice driven by passion for patient care has grown into one of the region's most trusted rehabilitation centers, serving thousands of patients annually.

Our founders recognized a critical gap in the healthcare landscape — the need for a clinic that combines world-class clinical expertise with genuinely personalized patient care. Too many patients were receiving generic treatment plans that failed to address the root cause of their conditions. Tadawi was created to change that.

Over the years, we have built a reputation grounded in measurable outcomes, evidence-based practice, and an unwavering commitment to each patient's unique recovery journey. Every member of our team shares the same belief: that every patient deserves the opportunity to move better, feel better, and live better.

Why Tadawi

The Tadawi Difference

We are not just another physiotherapy clinic. Here is what sets us apart and makes us the preferred choice for thousands of patients.

Board-Certified Specialists

Our therapists hold advanced degrees and international certifications, bringing specialized expertise across orthopedic, neurological, and sports rehabilitation disciplines.

State-of-the-Art Facility

Our clinic is equipped with the latest rehabilitation technology including laser therapy, shockwave devices, electrotherapy units, and a fully equipped exercise rehabilitation gym.

Advanced Treatment Techniques

We utilize manual therapy, dry needling, shockwave therapy, instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization, and other cutting-edge techniques to accelerate recovery.

Flexible Scheduling

We understand busy lifestyles. That is why we offer extended clinic hours including evenings and weekends, with multiple booking channels for your convenience.

Comprehensive Care Pathways

From initial assessment through full recovery, our structured treatment pathways ensure consistent, measurable progress at every stage of your rehabilitation.
