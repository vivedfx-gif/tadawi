General Questions

What conditions does Tadawi Clinic treat?

We treat a wide range of musculoskeletal and neurological conditions including back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, ACL rehabilitation, post-surgery recovery, stroke rehabilitation, shoulder pain, sciatica, disc problems, knee pain, pediatric conditions, women's health issues, and more. Our team uses evidence-based approaches to develop personalized treatment plans for each patient.

What makes Tadawi Clinic different?

Tadawi Clinic stands out through our evidence-based approach, personalized treatment plans, modern equipment, experienced certified therapists, and continuous follow-up. We combine clinical excellence with compassionate care to deliver the best outcomes for our patients, ensuring every treatment is tailored to individual needs and recovery goals.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we do accept walk-in patients subject to availability. However, we strongly recommend booking an appointment in advance through our website, WhatsApp, phone, or Facebook page to guarantee your preferred time slot and minimize waiting time.

Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through multiple channels: our website booking form, via WhatsApp by sending us a message, by calling us directly, or through our Facebook page. We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to accommodate your availability.

What are your working hours?

Our clinic operates from Saturday to Thursday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. On Fridays, we are open from 2:00 PM to 9:00 PM. We are closed on public holidays. Evening and weekend appointments are available for your convenience.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment with at least 24 hours' notice. This allows us to offer the time slot to other patients. You can cancel or reschedule via WhatsApp, phone call, or by contacting our team directly.

Treatment

How long does a typical treatment session last?

A typical treatment session lasts between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the nature and complexity of your condition. Your therapist will inform you of the expected duration during your initial assessment and adjust as your treatment progresses.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your specific condition, severity, and individual response to treatment. During your first visit, our therapists will evaluate your condition and provide a personalized treatment plan with a recommended number of sessions and estimated timeline for recovery.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you do not need a doctor's referral to visit Tadawi Clinic. You can book an appointment directly. However, if you have a doctor's referral or any relevant medical records, please bring them along as they can help our therapists develop a more comprehensive treatment plan.

What should I bring to my first visit?

For your first visit, please bring: any medical reports or doctor's notes, imaging results (X-rays, MRI, CT scans), a list of current medications and dosages, your insurance card if applicable, and comfortable clothing suitable for physical examination and exercises. Arriving 10-15 minutes early is recommended for paperwork.

Insurance & Payment

Do you accept insurance?

We work with a range of insurance providers. Please contact us directly or check with your insurance company to verify if Tadawi Clinic is within your network. Our team can assist you with any insurance-related questions and help you understand your coverage options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience: cash, credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfer. Payment is expected at the time of service. For treatment packages, we offer flexible payment plans — ask our front desk for details.

Clinic Policies

Is parking available?

Yes, there are nearby parking facilities available for our patients. Our clinic is located in an area with convenient parking options. If you need directions or parking recommendations, please contact us before your visit.

Is the clinic wheelchair accessible?

Absolutely. Our clinic is fully wheelchair accessible with ramp access, wide doorways, accessible restrooms, and treatment areas designed to accommodate patients with mobility challenges. We are committed to providing equal access to all patients.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes, we offer selective home visit programs for patients who are unable to travel to the clinic due to severe mobility limitations or medical conditions. Home visits are subject to availability and assessment. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation and determine if a home visit program is suitable for you.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us and we'll be happy to answer any additional questions you may have about our services.
